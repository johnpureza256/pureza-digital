Our coffee

A small corner of East Street,
dialled in every morning.

Triangle Espresso started with a three-group machine, a borrowed grinder and a stubborn belief that a country town deserves city coffee. Years on, that hasn't changed — just the queue out the door.

We pull shots of seasonal, single-origin beans roasted just up the road, and we taste-test every batch before it touches your cup. Slow mornings, good company, and a brew worth the drive across the plains.
